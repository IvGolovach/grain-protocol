# Code of Conduct

Grain is an open infrastructure project. We expect professional behavior.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but require technical clarity.
- Disagree on ideas, not people.
- Keep security discussions responsible (see SECURITY.md).

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Threats or incitement of violence.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in bans from project spaces.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues to: **conduct@grainprotocol.org** (replace with your real address).
